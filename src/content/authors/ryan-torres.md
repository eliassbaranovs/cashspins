His journalism background shows in the way he structures a story: lead with the most important fact, explain why it matters, give the reader enough context to form their own opinion, and get out. When a major operator pulls out of a market or a regulator announces new deposit limits, Ryan is the one explaining what it actually means for players — not just what the press release says, but what the second-order effects look like six months from now. He's particularly good at connecting dots between regulatory moves in different jurisdictions. His writing sits somewhere between a tech blog and a financial news desk: informed, slightly opinionated, accessible without being dumbed down."
