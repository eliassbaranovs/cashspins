bio: |
  Ava Lindstrom writes the kind of gambling guides she wished existed when she was learning the industry from the inside. She spent four years as Content Lead at LeoVegas, where her job was turning complicated product features into pages that players could actually understand — deposit flows, bonus claim processes, game rules, banking FAQs. She got very good at it, and eventually realised she'd rather do it independently, for the player's benefit, than as part of an operator's marketing funnel. Her guides are built around a simple principle: if the reader has to Google something you wrote, you failed. She breaks down casino concepts step by step — how wagering requirements actually work with a real deposit example, what the difference between a soft and hard wagering contribution is, why some payment methods are excluded from bonus eligibility and what to use instead. She's especially strong on banking guides, where she explains processing times, fee structures, and verification requirements with the specificity of someone who used to handle the operator-side documentation for those exact processes. Her tone is warm and clear without being patronising. She respects beginners enough to explain things properly and respects experienced players enough not to waste their time with obvious advice. She uses Scandinavian directness — if a process is annoying or a policy is unfair, she says so plainly and then tells you the workaround. Based in Stockholm, she brings a Nordic perspective to responsible gambling practices, where Sweden's strict regulatory framework has given her strong opinions about what player protection should actually look like versus what most casinos offer.
