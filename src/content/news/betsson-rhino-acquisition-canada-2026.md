Betsson officially completed its acquisition of Rhino Entertainment's Canadian operations by April 27, 2026. This transaction marks a strategic expansion for Betsson into the broader North American market. The company intends to integrate Rhino Entertainment's established infrastructure and existing customer base across Canada. This strategic maneuver reflects the ongoing consolidation and growth patterns observed within the iGaming industry.

The acquisition aims to provide Betsson with an immediate presence and operational capabilities in the Canadian market. Betsson views the Rhino Entertainment acquisition as a move consistent with its strategy to generate shareholder value. Betsson anticipates the deal will create economies of scale, strengthen profitability, and broaden growth opportunities across its B2C and B2B segments.

Rhino Entertainment indicated that divesting its B2C operations unlocks substantial value. This allows the company to concentrate on its next phase of growth.
