# Paysafe Launches 'Pay with Crypto' for US iGaming and Daily Fantasy Sports in 2026

Paysafe has introduced a new payment solution named "Pay with Crypto" for iGaming and daily fantasy sports operators in the United States, effective April 10, 2026. This development allows players to fund their accounts using various cryptocurrencies and stablecoins, which are then automatically converted to US dollars for gameplay. The solution aims to enhance payment flexibility and transaction speed for the growing US online gambling market.

## Paysafe Pay with Crypto US iGaming 2026: Key Details of the New Solution

Paysafe officially launched its "Pay with Crypto" solution on April 10, 2026, targeting iGaming and daily fantasy sports operators across the United States. This new offering enables players to deposit funds directly into their gaming accounts using a range of cryptocurrencies and stablecoins. A core feature of the service is the automatic conversion of these digital assets into US dollars before gameplay commences.

The technical infrastructure behind this solution is powered by MoonPay, a prominent crypto payment infrastructure provider. Players can connect their existing crypto wallets to facilitate deposits, streamlining the funding process. This integration means operators can offer a compliant and user-friendly crypto deposit option, with all gameplay occurring in traditional USD, mitigating volatility risks for both players and platforms.
