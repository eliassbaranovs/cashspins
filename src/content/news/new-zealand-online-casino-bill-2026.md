# New Zealand Online Casino Bill 2026 Passes Final Reading, Royal Assent Expected

New Zealand's Online Casino Gambling Bill has successfully passed its third and final reading in Parliament, moving towards Royal Assent and expected to become law in May 2026. This landmark legislation aims to regulate the online casino market, introduce a licensing framework for up to 15 operators, and significantly enhance consumer protections for players across the country.

## Key Details of the New Zealand Online Casino Bill 2026

The Online Casino Gambling Bill has successfully passed its third and final reading in the New Zealand Parliament, marking a pivotal step towards a regulated online casino market. It is expected to become law in May 2026 after receiving Royal Assent. This new legislation authorizes the issuance of up to 15 online casino licenses through a competitive application process.

Operators currently serving New Zealand players must apply for a license by December 1, 2026, or cease services. Non-compliant operators face substantial financial penalties, with fines reaching up to NZ$5 million. The bill also mandates that all licensed operators pay taxes and adhere to enhanced consumer protection standards. These measures will create a structured and safer environment for online gambling.

## Context and Background: New Zealand's iGaming Regulation Journey

This legislation will significantly impact online gambling operators by requiring them to obtain a license, pay taxes, and adhere to enhanced consumer protection standards. Operators failing to comply by December 1, 2026, face severe penalties. The move represents a comprehensive shift from an unregulated offshore market to a controlled domestic one.

Industry players have reacted to the upcoming changes. Entain, which operates TAB NZ, has indicated interest in securing up to three of the 15 available licenses. This bill follows earlier regulatory adjustments in New Zealand. The country increased its offshore gambling duty from 12% to 16%, with the additional 4% specifically allocated for community returns, effective July 1, 2024. A ban on offshore sports and racing betting also came into effect on June 28, 2025, granting TAB NZ a monopoly in this sector. ## What the New Zealand Gambling Law Means for Online Casino Players

The new law is expected to significantly impact online casino players in New Zealand by offering enhanced consumer protections. Licensed operators will be required to implement strict harm prevention measures. These include excluding problem gamblers, providing clear bonus terms, guaranteeing payouts, and offering mandatory deposit limit tools.
